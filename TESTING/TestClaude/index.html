Recent Buffs, Nerfs and Fixes

Patch Date: February 5, 2025

Weapons

Buffs

Cerberus (Buff)

Cervix reinforced. Tighter, stronger, faster recovery—still takes a pounding.

  • Increased damage (9 → 10 per pellet)
  • Tighter pellet spread for better accuracy
  • Reduced reload times

Pike-556 (Buff)

More penetration. Now they really feel it.

  • Increased damage (47 → 50)

SR-84 (Buff)

Faster bolt action, so you can shoot another load quicker.

  • Faster bolt action animation (1.25s → 1.05s), increasing fire rate
  • Increased damage (115 → 118)

Sword (Buff/Revert)

Length restored. Still waiting on the girth patch.

  • Increased secondary attack hit sweep length by 55cm (restored previous range)

Nerfs

FAMAS (Nerf)

No more Fast Ass Mowing And Spraying—you gotta last longer now.

  • Reduced fire rate (230 → 220 RPM)
  • Reduced damage per bullet (24 → 23)

LH1 (Nerf)

From Lethal Hit 1-shot to Lightly Hugs 1-shot. Weak.

  • Reduced damage (48 → 46)
  • Reduced fire rate (280 → 270 RPM)

Model 1887 (Nerf)

Shotgunners were clapping too hard, so we nerfed their cheeks.

  • Reduced damage (13 → 11 per pellet)
  • Min damage falloff multiplier increased (0.6 → 0.7), keeping same max-range damage as in 5.7

Quality of Life Adjustments

CL-40 (QoL Buff)

Less self-damage because y’all kept blowing yourselves too soon.

  • Reduced projectile radius (10cm → 5cm) to prevent accidental surface hits
  • Lowered self-damage multiplier (1.6 → 1.25)

Dagger (QoL Adjustment)

Buffed for Back Shots History Month—now hits deeper from behind.

  • Added FoV change during secondary attack charge-up for better clarity

  • Reduced throw delay (0.9s → 0.6s)

Nerfs

Winch Claw (Nerf)

Specializations

Nerfs

No more one-shot, one-thrust. Takes longer to finish now.

Props disappear quicker—clean up your damn mess.

  • Now removes props attached to wall segments for cleaner passageways
