Weapons

Balance Changes

Dagger (Adjustment)

  • Reduced backstab angle on secondary attack from 180° to 150° to address unintended backstabs.

KS-23 (Buff)

  • Increased minimum damage falloff multiplier (0.6 → 0.7) to improve long-range effectiveness.
  • Decreased bullet dispersion when aiming down sights, enhancing accuracy across various stances.

Developer Notes

The Dagger adjustment aims to reduce instances of unintended backstabs. The KS-23 changes are intended to improve its viability following previous updates.

Starting with Season 6, balance updates will occur approximately every three weeks for a more consistent gameplay experience.
