📊 How These Rankings Are Built

Our weapon meta is crafted from a blend of raw performance stats, real player data, and community sentiment. We pull from multiple sources—including pick/win rates from TheFinals.gg, TTK/DPS calculations from spreadsheets, and our own gameplay analysis. Our tier system also reflects how weapons synergize with specs and gadgets in real-world chaos, not just in spreadsheets.

We also factor in community frustration and usage patterns to help surface weapons that are strong, tricky, or just plain troll-worthy. The result: a tier list that balances theorycrafting, meta sweats, and actual in-game madness.

🔑 KEY TAKEAWAYS - PATCH 7.6.0

• KS-23 rises from worst to viable (hitscan ≤ 25 m)

• Minigun buff pushes it to A tier - walls melt, heals sustain

• Riot Shield jumps to A tier after hit-detection overhaul

• Sledge nerfs drop it from S tier but still one-shot tool in ambush

• FCAR becomes pro favorite for Medium - no falloff, brutal DPS

• Healing meta buffs burst weapons - XP-54, 93R, SA-1216 flourish
