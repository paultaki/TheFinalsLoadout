Light Class Weapons

High mobility, close-range dominance

S-TIER Meta Defining

XP-54 SMG

🟨 STABLE
S

DPS: ~229 | Fire Rate: 860 RPM | TTK: 0.64/1.06/1.48s (L/M/H)

Full TTK ⓘ
Time to Kill:
🟦 Light (150HP): 0.64s
🟪 Medium (250HP): 1.06s
🟨 Heavy (350HP): 1.48s

The undisputed Light GOAT. Low recoil, versatile range, easy to use. Nerfed in S7: 17→16 damage. Still meta despite damage nerf.

Overall Rating: 95/100

ARN-220 Carbine

📈 RISING
S

DPS: ~227 | TTK: 0.66/1.10/1.54s (L/M/H)

The new king? Recoil normalization buff made it laser-accurate. Fantastic range advantage over XP-54. Many pros now call it "stupid strong." Dual magazine system (0.75s swap).

✨ BUFFED: Recoil normalized

Overall Rating: 96/100

M11 SMG

⚡ HIGH SKILL
S

DPS: ~267 | Fire Rate: 1000 RPM | TTK: 0.54/0.90/1.26s (L/M/H)

Highest Light DPS at 1000 RPM, extreme recoil. Raw stats say S-tier, community says high-A due to difficulty.

⚠️ Extreme recoil | ✅ Highest Light DPS

Overall Rating: 88/100

93R Burst Pistol

V9-S Pistol

Heavy Hitter
A

Damage: 40/shot | TTK: 0.50/0.75/1.25s (L/M/H)

The .50 cal handcannon. Blazing fast TTK rewards accuracy. High recoil keeps it from S-tier. Silenced - reduced enemy detection.

SH-1900 Double Barrel

CQB Only
A

LH1 Pistol

Medium Class Weapons

S-TIER Meta Defining

FAMAS Burst Rifle

📈 NEW META
S

Fire Rate: 700 RPM | TTK: 0.76/1.27/1.77s (L/M/H) | Fire Mode: 3-round burst

THE Medium meta pick. Virtually no recoil with red dot. Laser headshots at range, solid hip-fire up close. Multiple buffs = dominance.
