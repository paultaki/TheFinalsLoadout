Balance Changes That Matter

💥 CL-40 Nerf
NERF
Self-damage increased: 25% → 35%
Max damage radius reduced: 60cm → 50cm
💡 What this means

Can't spam at your feet as effectively, need better aim for full damage

🎮 Why it matters

Less dominant against clustered teams around Healing Emitters

🏥 Healing Emitter Rework
REWORK
Cooldown starts when destroyed (not on deployment)
Can retrieve it: Manual pickup = 3s cooldown, Remote = 15s cooldown
Base cooldown: 35s → 30s
💡 What this means

Smart placement now crucial - can't just drop and forget

🎮 Impact

More tactical deployable management, rewards good positioning

💉 H+ Infuser Nerf
NERF
Healing per shot: 20 → 15
💡 What this means

25% less healing, slower cashout steal support

🎮 Impact

Still viable but less oppressive during objective play

💨 Smoke Grenades Counter-Play Fixed
BUG FIX
Bug fixed: Tracking Dart, Sonar Grenade, and Proximity Sensor now properly reveal enemies through smoke
💡 What this means

Smoke no longer blocks detection gadgets

🎮 Impact

Major indirect nerf to smoke meta - detection gadgets are now hard counters
