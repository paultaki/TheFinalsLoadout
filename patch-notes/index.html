Patch Notes Summary

Buffs • Nerfs • Updates

Recent Buffs, Nerfs, and Adjustments from The Finals Patch Notes

PATCH DATE: MARCH 19, 2025

Weapons

Buffs
  • M60: Damage increased (19→20), improved effective range.
  • Dagger: Increased base and secondary attack damage.
  • KS-23: Faster pump-action, increased RPM.
  • XP-54: Damage increased, effective range extended.
  • CL-40: Increased blast radius and projectile speed.
Nerfs
  • LH1: Damage decreased from 46 to 40.
  • SA1216: Reduced pellet count, adjusted damage and pattern.
  • Pike-556: Reduced effective range and visual recoil.
Adjustments
  • AKM & FAMAS: Improved recoil smoothness.
  • M11: Slightly increased effective range, increased falloff.
New Weapons
  • The Eraser: Heavy railgun; high damage, slow reload.
  • V9S Silenced SMG: Light class stealth SMG.

Gadgets

Buffs
  • Anti-Gravity Cube: Cooldown reduced, radius increased.
  • APS Turret: Increased durability, blocks additional projectile.
  • Tracking Dart: Increased tracking duration.
Nerfs
  • Data Reshaper: Ammo count reduced.
  • Gravity Vortex: Decreased throw range and velocity.
Adjustments
  • Glitch Grenade: Fuse time decreased.
  • Explosive Canisters: Fuse time decreased.
New Gadget
  • Echo Decoy: Deployable holographic decoy.

Specializations

Buffs
  • Winch Claw: Increased range, dynamic stun duration.
Nerfs
  • Cloaking Device: Increased visibility, delayed cloaking activation.

Additional Notes: Weapon Sights

  • Holographic: ARN-220, AKM, Famas, CB-01, LH1, M60, Lewis Gun, M11, XP-54, Matter.
  • Looking Glass: ShAK, ARN-220, AKM, Famas, FCAR, CB-01, LH1, M60, Lewis Gun, XP-54, Matter, Model 1887.
  • Adder Reflex: ShAK, ARN-220, AKM, Famas, FCAR, CB-01, LH1, M60, Lewis Gun, XP-54, Matter.
  • Compact Reflector: ShAK, ARN-220, Famas, FCAR, CB-01, XP-54.
