Weapon Balance Changes

KS-23

HYBRID WEAPON HIGH IMPACT

Now a hybrid weapon - hitscan up to 25m, projectile beyond. Best of both worlds.

0-25m range: Hitscan (instant)
25m+ range: Projectile (travel time)

💥 Meta Impact: More reliable at close-medium range while maintaining long-range potential.

Sword

DAMAGE BUFF MEDIUM IMPACT

Damage buffed significantly but lunge rotation clamped to prevent 180° turns.

Primary damage: 74 → 88 (+19%)
Secondary damage: 105 → 120 (+14%)
Lunge rotation: Clamped to 40°

⚔️ Meta Impact: More lethal in direct combat but requires better positioning.

Defibrillator

NERF LOW IMPACT

Slightly slower charge time for more balanced revives.

Charge time: 0.8s → 1.0s (+25%)

💉 Meta Impact: 0.2s longer revives give enemies more counterplay opportunity.
