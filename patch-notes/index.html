Patch Notes Summary

Recent buffs, nerfs, and fixes from The Finals patch notes.

PATCH 5.12.0 - MARCH 6, 2025

Weapons

Model 1887

  • Increased damage per pellet from 11 to 12.
  • Decreased lever-action animation duration from 0.85s to 0.8s, effectively increasing the fire rate from 70 RPM to 75 RPM.

*Developer Note:* These adjustments aim to restore the Model 1887's effectiveness after previous balance changes.

Recurve Bow

  • Increased maximum draw damage from 120 to 124. Base damage remains at 60.
