Weapon Overhaul

CL-40

NERF HIGH IMPACT

The explosive powerhouse gets accuracy and self-damage nerfs to prevent feet-shot abuse.

Self-damage multiplier: 1.25x → 1.35x (+35%)
Inner blast radius: 60cm → 50cm

💥 Meta Impact: Splashing at your feet now costs ~20 more HP. Still melts Healing Emitter bunkers—just costs you blood.

H+ Infuser

NERF MEDIUM IMPACT

25% less healing makes pocket medic plays less oppressive during cashout steals.

Healing per shot: 20 → 15 (-25%)

💉 Meta Impact: Flat healing per second reduced. Less oppressive during crucial moments.

Healing Emitter

REWORK HIGH IMPACT

Smart placement now crucial - can't drop and forget anymore. Cooldown starts when destroyed.

Cooldown start: On Deploy → On Destroy
Manual retrieval: 3s cooldown
Remote retrieval: 15s cooldown

🏥 Meta Impact: Smart placement critical. Lazy deployments backfire.

Sledgehammer

BUFF MEDIUM IMPACT

Fewer ghost swings - melee finally feels consistent with improved hit detection.

Primary attack height: 15cm → 25cm (+67%)

🔨 Gameplay Impact: 67% taller hit detection means fewer frustrating misses.

⚡ Weapon Draw/Holster Speed Changes

✅ Faster Swaps

  • 93R: 0.20s → 0.15s (both)
  • XP-54: 0.23s → 0.20s

🐌 Slower Swaps

  • AKM: 0.23s → 0.30s equip
  • FCAR: 0.20s → 0.30s equip
  • FAMAS: 0.20s → 0.30s equip
  • Lewis Gun: 0.23s → 0.35s (heaviest)
  • LH1: 0.15s → 0.30s (doubled)
  • Model 1887: 0.15s → 0.30s (doubled)
  • SR-84: 0.15s → 0.30s (doubled)

Impact: Quick-swap playstyles nerfed. Commit to your primary weapon.
