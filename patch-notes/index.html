Patch Notes FAQ (Season 7)

What's new in The Finals Season 7?

Season 7 introduces three new specializations (H+ Infuser, Breach Drill, Healing Emitter), a new map called Nozomi/Citadel, the Practice Range, XP Dash event, and multiple balance changes including KS-23 buffs and XP-54 nerfs.

Where can I find the best loadouts after the Season 7 update?

Check out our Meta Weapons & Loadouts guide for the latest Season 7 tier lists, class-specific builds, and weapon rankings based on updated stats and player feedback.

What weapons got nerfed or buffed in v7.0?

XP-54 was nerfed (damage and RPM adjustment), while KS-23, ARN-220, and melee weapons received buffs. See the full weapon balance section for details.

When did The Finals Season 7 go live?

Season 7 launched on June 11, 2025. It's titled "The Divide" and is available across all platforms with crossplay support.
