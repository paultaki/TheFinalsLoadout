🧠 Slang School w/ Prof. FAT KUSH

Stay

Means "am at" → "I stay at work." Hawaiian slang vibes. First lecture delivered by the professor himself.

Cherry

Means clean, smooth, or good. "That kill was cherry." Has become server law and a quality metric.

Kenneth

Formal variant of Kenny. Used when logging in with extra authority or after a significant absence.

W

Victory, success, or approval. "W confirmed." Can be used even when not present for the actual W.
