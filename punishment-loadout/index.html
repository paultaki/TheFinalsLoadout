Frequently Asked Questions

What is this generator?

This is a randomizer that creates unique loadouts for The Finals game, combining different weapons, specializations, and gadgets across all three weight classes.

How does it work?

Click the "Generate Your Punishment" button and watch as it randomly selects a class, weapon, specialization, and three gadgets. The spinning animation adds suspense to your impending doom.

Are these loadouts viable?

Absolutely not! That's part of the fun. These combinations are designed to be challenging and possibly hilarious. Success with these loadouts is not guaranteed (or likely).

Can I share my loadout?

Yes! Click the "Copy Loadout" button after generation to copy the loadout to your clipboard. Share your suffering with friends!

Why would I use this?

Because sometimes you want chaos. Sometimes you want to challenge yourself. Sometimes you just want to make your teammates question your life choices.

Is this an official The Finals tool?

No, this is a fan-made tool created for entertainment purposes. All game content and properties belong to Embark Studios.
