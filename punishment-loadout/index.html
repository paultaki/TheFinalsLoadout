What is a Punishment Loadout?

A Punishment Loadout is a randomized selection of weapons and gadgets meant to challenge you with unconventional and often suboptimal setups.

Can I customize my loadout?

Nope! The whole point is to test your adaptability with whatever you get.

Why would I use this?

Because it's hilarious, chaotic, and a great way to push your limits as a player.

Is this an official tool?

Nope! This is a fan-made challenge mode for those who love suffering in The Finals.
